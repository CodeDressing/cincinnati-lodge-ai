Event Classification System

Three Categories for Long-Term Growth

Every event should have a clear purpose. Some programs support Lodge sustainability, some build public trust and community presence, and others strengthen brotherhood, education, and member culture.

01

Revenue Events

Ticketed programs, entertainment nights, networking events, holiday events, sponsorship opportunities, and rental-driven programming that helps support Lodge operations.

02

Community Events

Public-facing programs that build goodwill, strengthen visibility, connect the Lodge with Morristown residents, and present the building as a positive civic gathering place.

03

Masonic Events

Member-focused programs that support brotherhood, education, leadership development, ritual culture, fellowship, and long-term Lodge strength.
